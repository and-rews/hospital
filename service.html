In-Patient Services

At Atasemanso Hospital, we provide 24 hour per day care. Our experienced staff serves our patients who are hospitalized to receive specialized medical and surgical treatments and procedures that are not manageable as an outpatient. Our unit has 20 beds, including several private rooms for patients requiring isolation.

Nursing care is based upon respect for the dignity and worth of every individual. We believe in quality care, professionalism, caring, flexibility, commitment and continuous improvement.

In order to provide all the best for our inpatients, Atasemanso Hospital provides a physician in our facility 24 hours per day, seven days per week.

Out-Patient Consultancy

We provide outpatient services medical procedures or tests that can be done in our medical center without an overnight stay. Many procedures and tests can be done in a few hours. Outpatient services include:

  • Wellness and prevention, such as counseling and weight-loss programs.
  • Diagnosis, such as lab tests and MRI scans.
  • Treatment, such as some surgeries.
  • Rehabilitation, such as drug or alcohol rehab and physical therapy.

What are the benefits of choosing outpatient services? Our Outpatient services usually cost less, because you don't need to stay overnight. Staff members at outpatient centers are well trained in the service they provide. All the care you need can be provided in one place.

In order to provide all the best for our inpatients, Atasemanso Hospital provides a physician in our facility 24 hours per day, seven days per week.

Laboratory Services

Antenatal & Post Natal

Prenatal care helps decrease risks during pregnancy and increases the chance of a safe and healthy delivery. Regular prenatal visits can help your doctor monitor your pregnancy and identify any problems or complications before they become serious. In view of this, we provide you with only the best doctors to take care of you during and after pregnancy. Our doctors offer special classes at different stages of your pregnancy, these classes will:

  • discuss what to expect when you are pregnant
  • prepare you for the birth
  • teach you basic skills for caring for your baby

Health Screening

At Atasemanso Hospital, we offer screening tests that look for diseases before you have symptoms. These screening tests can find diseases early, when they're easier to treat. Our highly specialized doctors and our special equipment, will help detect any form of hidden diseases and treat it even before you have the symptoms. Some conditions that we commonly screen for include:

  • Breast cancer and cervical cancer in women
  • Colorectal cancer
  • Diabetes
  • High blood pressure
  • High cholesterol
  • Osteoporosis
  • Overweight and obesity
  • Prostate cancer in men

Ultrasound

Our ultrasound imaging uses high-frequency sound waves to look at organs and structures inside your body and our health care professionals use it to view the heart, blood vessels, kidneys, liver, and other organs. During pregnancy, doctors use ultrasound to view the fetus. Unlike x-rays, ultrasound does not expose you to radiation. We specialize in:

  • General Ultrasound Imaging
  • Lung Ultrasound
  • Abdominal Ultrasound
  • Obstetrical Ultrasound
  • Sonohysterography
