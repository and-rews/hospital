About Us

Best Medical Care For Yourself and Your Family

Atasemanso Hospital Limited was established on 1 July 1995 at Sepe Buokrom and was moved to the Atasemanso Township on the January 1, 2001. The Hospital manned by DR John Boateng, who has BSC, MB and CHB is the managing Director of the Hospital. The Deputy Director is in the person of MRS. Rita Boateng. The Hospital has competent Surgeons who perform successful surgical operations.
