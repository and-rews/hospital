Excellent Medical Services

Out-Patient Consultancy

We provide outpatient services medical procedures or tests that can be done in our medical center without an overnight stay. Many procedures and tests can be done in a few hours. Outpatient services include:

  1. Wellness and prevention, such as counseling and weight-loss programs.
  2. Diagnosis, such as lab tests and MRI scans.
  3. Treatment, such as some surgeries.
  4. Rehabilitation, such as drug or alcohol rehab and physical therapy.

What are the benefits of choosing outpatient services?

Our Outpatient services usually cost less, because you don't need to stay overnight. Staff members at outpatient centers are well trained in the service they provide. All the care you need can be provided in one place.

Academic Attachment

Various health institutes send out students for their (ROTATIONS) practical learning experience ranging from 4weeks to 12weeks duration. Atasomanso having very effective facilities are able to accommodate these students successfully. The institution includes Komfo Anokye Teaching Hospital, Kwame Nkrumah University Of Science And Technology, University Of Ghana-Legon, Mampong midwifery training school, Salvation Army Hospital, Leyson Hospital ST Gibrine Hospital and others. The students are in the grade of degree, diploma course whilst some are on the job training grades.

A private organization known as PROJECT ABROAD constantly sent students from overseas to acquire experience in Atasomanso Hospital, which is very fast catching up. Testimonials are issued out to them for their association with Atasomanso Hospital.

Delivery & Post Natal Care

Prenatal care helps decrease risks during pregnancy and increases the chance of a safe and healthy delivery. Regular prenatal visits can help your doctor monitor your pregnancy and identify any problems or complications before they become serious.

In view of this, we provide you with only the best doctors to take care of you during and after pregnancy. Our doctors offer special classes at different stages of your pregnancy, these classes will:

  1. discuss what to expect when you are pregnant
  2. prepare you for the birth
  3. teach you basic skills for caring for your baby

It’s important to stay as healthy as possible during pregnancy and during the postpartum period. Stay on top of all of your healthcare appointments and follow your doctor’s instructions for the health and safety of you and your baby.
